Company Introduce · 公司介绍

Anhui Eastern Communication Imp. & Exp. Co., Ltd.

安徽东渐进出口有限公司 / AEC GROUP

Anhui Eastern Communication Imp.& Exp. Co., Ltd Combines production, research, development, sales and service.Company focuses on exporting-oriented business. Company started from flexible plastic packaging film,after years of operation,grew as the one which mainly focuses on the flexible packaging films,(BOPP,BOPET,POF,BOPS,BOPA,CPP,PE..) film products of deep processing such as:printing color,cutting into smaller rolls and sheets, metallized aluminum,coating difference glues, etc.,office printing supplies (was/resin thermal transfer printing ribbons, adhesive labels), new energy battery usage of thin film,film-relatives automation equipment and accessories;office paper supplies, photo & copy inkjet paper products etc.

Address: No.1158 Huizhou Ave., Baohe Industrial District, 230051, Hefei City, Anhui Province, P.R. China

安徽东渐进出口有限公司生产制造和经营进出口的产品覆盖软包装塑膜工业、涂布工业、印刷复合业，镀铝镭射业等。公司从软塑料包装薄膜及制品业起步，经过多年的经营，成长为一家，在包装薄膜（BOPP聚丙烯薄膜，BOPET聚酯薄膜，POF聚烯烃热收缩膜，BOPS聚苯乙烯薄膜，PE聚乙烯薄膜），薄膜及其制品的深加工（镀铝、彩印、涂布、分切等），薄膜电子材料 (电容膜，碳带膜，新能源电池膜），胶粘带，拆封拉线，袋子、塑料包扎制品和部分自动化机器设备及配件；在办公用打印碳带，标签，复印纸，相纸，打印耗材等行业，具有独特的优势。

安徽东渐主营软塑料包装薄膜工业，和薄膜深加工系列工业，镀铝，涂布工业，胶水，办公用打印碳带，标签，转印、打印耗材系列等。

地址：中国安徽省合肥市包河工业区徽州大道1158号，邮编230051
