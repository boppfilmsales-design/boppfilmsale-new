Product Description · 产品描述
POF (Polyolefin) Thermal Heat Shrinkable Cling Film — an environmentally-friendly five-layer co-extruded packaging film. Types available: • POF cling shrink film – Center Folded (e.g. 19mic × 45cm × 1000m; 30mic × 585mm × 1500m) • POF cling shrink film – Tubular Form (e.g. 25mic × 330mm × 1000m) • POF macro perforated cling film Thickness: 12–25 microns Main Features: • High-resolution, glossy transparent appearance; protects products from dust, moisture and odor • Excellent anti-crack toughness and high anti-impact strength; stable under high and low temperatures; ideal for frozen product transport • Fast and high shrinking rate; applicable to various product shapes and sizes • Lightweight, uniform thickness, economical Applications: beverages, food, medicine, chemicals packaging 中文描述： POF（聚烯烃）热收缩缠绕膜——五层共挤环保包装膜，突破传统三层共挤工艺，具有更高韧性和抗冲击强度。 类型：中折型 / 管状型 / 打孔型 厚度：12–25微米 主要特点：高透明光泽，防尘防潮防异味；抗撕裂韧性强，高低温稳定，适合冷冻品运输；收缩率高，适用各种形状产品。 用途：饮料、食品、医药、化工品包装。
