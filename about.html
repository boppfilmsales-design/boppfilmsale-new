Company Profile · 公司简介

Anhui Eastern Communication Imp.& Exp. Co., Ltd is an integrated companies approved by China National Ministry of Foreign Trade and General Administration of Customs. The company has its own independent import and export rights. Registered in Hefei City, Anhui Province, P.R. China.

Anhui Eastern Communication Imp.& Exp. Co., Ltd combines production, research, development, sales and service, company focus on exporting oriented business. Company started from flexible plastic packaging film and after years of operation, grew as the one which mainly focuses on the flexible packaging films.

Anhui Eastern Communication Group mainly focus on flexible plastic BOPP, BOPET, CPP, BOPS, POF packaging film industry, BOPP thermal lamination EVA coating films, adhesive tape jumbo rolls, BOPS windows envelope film, office paper products and printing supplies series industry, machinery equipments and parts industry, etc.

Our main products diversified followings:

1. Flexible plastic packaging film industry

1.) BOPP film including BOPP plain film, BOPP flower wrapping film, BOPP foil, BOPP tape-grade film, BOPP clothes/bag-grade film, BOPP mini/smaller roll, BOPP anti-static film; BOPP tobacco film; BOPP heat sealable film; BOPP matte film; BOPP pearlized film; BOPP thermal film; BOPP capacitor film; BOPP Sheets; BOPP anti-fog film; BOPP micro/macro perforated film; Metallized BOPP film (VMBOPP); BOPP tear tape; BOPP laser film and so on.

2.) BOPET film including BOPP packing film, BOPET lamination film, BOPET insulation film, BOPET sheets, BOPET capacitor film, BOPET thermal transfer basic film, metallized BOPET film and so on.

3.) CPP film including CPP printing film, CPP laminating film, CPP bag film, retort cpp film, metallized cpp film and so on.

4.) BOPA film including BOPA printing and laminating film

5.) BOPS film including BOPS window envelop film and BOPS shrinkage film.

6.) POF shrinkage cling film; PVC cling film; PE cling film for food wrapping, PE stretch film for pallets packaging with machine/hand use, PE bags and so on.

2. Adhesive Packing Tape Industry

1.) BOPP adhesive tape clear and color (jumbo rolls and slit smaller rolls); 2.) Masking tapes, double side tape 3.) Color logo printing film 4.) Aluminum tape 5.) All kinds of bags 6.) PP sheets and sleeves and so on.

3. Coating film

1.) BOPP thermal lamination film with EVA coated, & BOPET/BOPA thermal lamination film with EVA coated 2.) PVDC coating film of BOPP/BOPET/BOPA 3.) Acrylic coating film

4. Printing inkjet supplies Series: Wax, resin Ribbon (jumbo rolls and small finished rolls), color Ribbon (jumbo rolls and small finished rolls), date tape (jumbo rolls and small finished rolls), self adhesive labels (including roll and pages), the Mexican round, printers, etc.

5. Wrap wire, tear clips and tear tape: plastic PP tear clips, self-adhesive tear tape, PP belt, and so on.

6. Paper products series and packaging paper, printing supplies series

1.) Office paper (a. Copy paper in reams and rolls b. LWC paper (lower/light weight-coated paper) and Coated paper c. Offset paper & coated paper. 2.) Printing supplies (a. Photo paper series and printing supplies series b. Outdoor advertising supplies series

The size of the Group's total investment (the sum of all branches) nearly 208 billion yuan (RMB, CNY), built 4 modern production plants, with a total area of 300 acres. Enter into industrial lines in the film (BOPP, BOPET, CPP, BOPA, BOPS, POF, PE, PVC and so on), glues, tapes, chemical coating, machinery & equipment, paper and other industries. The total number of employees reached 1,200, nearly 68 high-tech technician talents.

Today, company has iron strength in finance and economics and acts as critical role in exporting business. Additionally, company also has a unique advantage in further processing industrial products.

Anhui Eastern Communication adopts "national industries as cornerstone, global market-oriented as platform, and scientific development as road". As products crossed over the world, service known around the world, and benefit spreaded to all corners of the world.

公司概况

安徽东渐进出口有限公司是一家经工商管理局批准具有自主进出口经营权的企业。注册于中国安徽省合肥市。企业沐浴在浓浓的徽风皖韵之中，徜徉在雄雄的江淮走廊之下。南枕黄山曼妙尖新的天然胜景，东接沪宁繁华鼎盛的工商辐射，西望武汉北依蚌淮。长江水运得天独厚，省会优势日渐凸显。

安徽东渐是集生产，研发，销售和服务为一体的综合性对外经贸企业，公司从软塑料包装薄膜及制品业起步，经过多年的经营，成长为一家，在包装薄膜（BOPP聚丙烯薄膜，BOPET聚酯薄膜，POF聚烯烃热收缩膜，BOPS聚苯乙烯薄膜，PE聚乙烯薄膜），薄膜及其制品的深加工（镀铝、彩印、涂布、分切等），薄膜电子材料 (电容膜，碳带膜，新能源电池膜），胶粘带，拆封拉线，袋子、塑料包扎制品和部分自动化机器设备及配件；在办公用打印碳带，标签，复印纸，相纸，打印耗材等行业，具有独特的优势。另外，公司对加工贸易工业产品更具优势。

安徽东渐主营产品覆盖软塑料包装薄膜工业，薄膜深加工系列工业，薄膜涂布工业，胶水、纸制品（包括：办公、包装用纸用品和相纸、打印耗材系列）等。

软塑料包装薄膜工业主要包括：双拉BOPP薄膜（包装膜和电工膜），BOPET薄膜（包装膜和电工膜），CPP薄膜，BOPA薄膜，BOPS薄膜，PE薄膜，PVC/POF薄膜，PET热转印基膜等，以及上述薄膜的深加工产品，如：彩印、镀铝、涂布、分切、再包装等。

薄膜涂布工业主要包括：涂布胶黏带母卷（半成品）如：BOPP胶带母卷，美纹纸胶带母卷，双面胶带母卷；PVDC涂布膜，EVA涂布膜和丙烯酸涂布膜等。

塑料深加工工业产品主要包括：薄膜分切，薄膜彩印，薄膜涂布，薄膜镀铝，薄膜制袋等。

打印耗材系列：热转印碳带（含母卷和小卷），色带（含母卷和小卷），标签（含卷装和页装）等。

包扎线和拉线：塑料包扎绳，金属扎线和拆封拉线等。

胶水工业包括：涂布用胶水及原料包括丙烯酸丁酯，EVA胶水，PVDC胶水，和彩印用印刷色素及液体原料等。

纸制品工业主要包括：打印耗材用照片纸系列，户外广告耗材系列，灯箱布系列等；和办公用纸静电复印纸、轻量涂布纸、双胶纸、新闻纸、铜版纸等系列产品以及鲜花、礼品等包装纸、特种纸等。

机械设备工业主要包括：薄膜和胶带分切机系列，各类薄膜涂胶用涂布机系列，包装机和薄膜印刷机系列，薄膜复合，复卷机器系列，收缩机，包扎机，封口机以及上述各类机器配件等。

经过多年的经营实践和不断发展，公司客户众多、业绩卓著、队伍精干、效率领先。敏锐的把握国际市场需求面和中国外向型经济的供给面的矛盾发展；深刻的认识和理解国际产业资本，商品资本和金融资本的流动规律，特别是中国大陆"世界工厂"和各国家间，地区间，经济体间的互补工业资本流动特征。适时展开经济贸易关系，夯实经贸基础，做长期稳定的订单。

回顾过去，奋斗历程感天动地。展望未来，事业发展永无止境。

今天的安徽东渐进出口有限公司正以她无比自信的微笑迎接新的明天。

注：东渐公司——其中"东渐"取自中西方经济文化交流中的"东学西渐，西学东渐"之意。现在中国是全球制造业大国，需要继续承载和深化全球制造业大国的市场己任和影响。在此，"东渐"有两个方面意思，一方面指中国出口企业自强繁荣，强劲发展并不断深化；另一方面，在此过程中，实现国内企业走向世界，更好参与国际工业产业链分工，为全球经济健康发展做出积极贡献，以更高姿态屹立世界民族之林，"渐"即汇通。
