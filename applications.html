Industries We Serve

我们服务的行业领域

Our films serve packaging and product protection across a broad spectrum of global industries. 我们的薄膜广泛应用于全球各行各业的包装与产品保护领域。

🍔
Food Packaging · 食品包装
Snacks, confectionery, fresh food
Read more →
Our BOPP and BOPET films are widely used in food packaging applications including snack bags, confectionery wrappers, fresh produce packaging, and frozen food films. Our films provide excellent moisture barrier properties, high clarity, and superior printability. We offer food-grade films that comply with international food safety standards including FDA and EU regulations.
💊
Pharmaceutical · 医药
Blister, pouch, tablet packaging
Read more →
We provide specialized films for pharmaceutical packaging including blister packs, tablet strips, and medical device packaging. Our films offer excellent barrier properties to protect medications from moisture, oxygen, and light. We manufacture cleanroom-grade films that meet pharmaceutical industry standards.
🚬
Tobacco · 烟草
Cigarette film & overwrap
Read more →
Our tobacco packaging films include BOPP cigarette box wrapping films, shrink films, and tear tapes. We offer high-gloss and matte finishes with excellent printability for brand visibility. Our films provide superior moisture barrier properties to maintain product freshness and comply with tobacco industry regulations.
🖨️
Printing · 印刷出版
Books, magazines, covers
Read more →
Our films are widely used in the printing industry for book covers, magazine wrappers, posters, and promotional materials. We offer excellent printability with high ink adhesion, vibrant colors, and consistent quality. Our BOPP films provide the perfect surface for gravure, flexo, and digital printing processes.
💐
Floral & Gift · 花卉礼品
Bouquet wrap, gift packaging
Read more →
We offer a wide range of flower wrapping films including BOPP flower wrap, cellophane, and specialty films for bouquet packaging. Our films provide excellent clarity to showcase flowers while protecting them from moisture and damage. Available in various sizes, colors, and finishes for different floral arrangements.
💿
CD / DVD · 光碟
Disc packaging & overwrap
Read more →
Our films are used for CD and DVD packaging including disc sleeves, shrink wraps, and display cases. We offer crystal-clear films that showcase the disc artwork while providing protection from scratches and dust. Our films are also used for software packaging and media storage solutions.
📱
Electronics · 电子
Capacitor, insulation film
Read more →
We manufacture specialized films for the electronics industry including capacitor films, insulation films, and protective films for electronic components. Our BOPP capacitor films offer excellent dielectric properties, high breakdown voltage, and consistent thickness for reliable performance in electronic circuits.
☀️
Solar / PV · 光伏
Module backsheet
Read more →
Our BOPET films are used as backsheet materials for solar photovoltaic modules. These films provide excellent weather resistance, UV protection, and electrical insulation. They are designed to withstand harsh outdoor conditions for 25+ years, ensuring long-term reliability for solar energy systems.

Bottle label, barcode
Read more →
We offer high-quality label films for bottle labels, barcode labels, and product identification. Our films provide excellent printability, adhesion, and durability. Available in clear, white, and metallic finishes for various labeling applications including food bottles, beverage containers, and industrial products.

Personal Care · 日化
Soap, cosmetics packaging
Read more →
Our films are used for packaging personal care products including soap wrappers, cosmetic bags, and hygiene product packaging. We offer films with excellent clarity, moisture barrier properties, and printability. Our films help protect products from contamination while showcasing brand identity.

Industrial · 工业
Pallet wrap, protection
Read more →
We provide heavy-duty films for industrial applications including pallet wrap, protective films, and packaging for industrial components.
