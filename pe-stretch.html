Product Description · 产品描述
PE Pallet Stretch Film — blown PE stretch film engineered for pallet packaging. Characteristics: Excellent anti-piercing and stretching ability; lightweight; strong tenacity; outstanding puncture resistance; good self-adhesion. Thickness: 15–20μm (hand wrap); 15–25μm (machine wrap) Width: 50–750mm Grades: Hand use / Semi-automatic machine / Fully automatic machine Colors: Natural clear, black, blue, red, ivory-white Applications: Pallet wrapping, warehouse logistics, transport protection. 中文：PE栈板拉伸缠绕膜——吹塑成型，专为栈板包装设计。特点：优异的抗刺穿和拉伸性能；轻量化；强韧性；出色的抗穿刺性；良好的自粘性。厚度：15–50μm，宽度：50–750mm，适用手动和机器缠绕。
