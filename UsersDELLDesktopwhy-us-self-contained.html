Why Choose AEC GROUP?

为什么选择AEC集团？

We combine manufacturing depth with international export expertise and dedicated service. 我们将制造实力与国际出口经验完美结合。

01
Integrated Group Resources
集团一体化资源
AEC GROUP combines Anhui East New Materials (manufacturing) with Anhui Eastern Communication (export) — direct factory pricing, quality at source, single point of contact for all orders. 集团化运营，工厂直销，源头质控，一站式服务。
Read more →
Our integrated structure allows us to control every step of the production process, from raw material sourcing to final packaging. This vertical integration ensures consistent quality, faster lead times, and cost savings that we pass directly to our customers. With over 20 years of experience, we have established strong relationships with raw material suppliers and logistics partners worldwide.
02
Extensive Product Range
丰富产品系列
From 3μm capacitor film to 75μm flower wrap; BOPP, BOPET, PVDC, CPP, POF and more — consolidate your sourcing with one trusted supplier. 从3微米电容器膜到75微米花卉膜，一站式满足所有薄膜采购需求。
Read more →
Our product portfolio includes over 200 specifications across 9 major categories. We offer films ranging from 3μm to 75μm in thickness, with widths from 250mm to 10,000mm. Our product range includes plain, glossy, matte, heat-sealable, pearlized, metallized, and specialty films. We also offer custom formulations for specific applications.
03
Custom Specifications
定制规格
Manufacture and source to your exact width, thickness, core size, roll length and performance requirements. Samples before bulk order. 按客户要求定制所有规格，批量下单前可提供样品评估。
Read more →
We understand that every customer has unique requirements. Our technical team works closely with you to develop custom solutions tailored to your specific needs. We offer custom thickness, width, surface treatment, and performance characteristics. Our R&D team can also develop new formulations for specialized applications.
04
20+ Years Export Experience
20余年出口经验
Deep experience in international logistics, LC documentation, export certificates and regulatory compliance across every major global market. 深厚国际物流、信用证及合规处理经验，覆盖全球主要市场。
Read more →
With over 20 years of export experience, we have shipped our products to more than 50 countries worldwide. Our team is well-versed in international trade regulations, customs procedures, and documentation requirements. We handle all export documentation including commercial invoices, packing lists, certificates of origin, and letters of credit.
05
Competitive Pricing
极具竞争力的价格
By integrating manufacturing and export, we eliminate unnecessary middlemen. Anhui's industrial cost advantages mean genuinely competitive global pricing. 集团化消除中间环节，安徽工业成本优势，全球竞争力价格。

Our integrated business model allows us to offer highly competitive pricing without compromising on quality. By controlling the entire production chain from raw materials to finished products, we eliminate intermediary costs. Combined with Anhui's advantageous industrial costs and our efficient operations, we provide exceptional value to our global customers.
06
Responsive Service
高效响应服务
Enquiries answered promptly. Detailed quotations, technical data sheets and sample arrangements without unnecessary delay. We value every client relationship. 询盘快速响应，提供详细报价、技术资料及样品安排，珍视每一位客户。
